Get to know about Welkebenet SACCO

Welkebenet Saving and Credit Cooperative Organization was established in Dec 19, 2023 (09/04/2016 E.C.) in sheger city, Mana Abichu subsity, tufa muna woreda. Welkebenet SACCOS Ltd. is a legally registered and recognized organization with registration number 147/98 to work Tufa Muna woreda. It is a saving and credit cooperative established on Dec 19, 2023 G.C. (09/04/2016 E.C.) by Mr. Hasen Nugusu, visionary founder and chief executive Officer and his friends, with 83 (3 women and 80 men) members and 345000 initial capital
