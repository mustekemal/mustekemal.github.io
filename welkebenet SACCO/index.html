Mission, Vision and Objectives

Mission

Improve the socio-economic conditions of members and other people through mobilizing savings and creating access to credit and asset building using relevant technology and highly motivated staff in consideration of social governance aspects

Vision

To be a World-Class Saving and Credit Cooperative organization by 2030 .

Objectives

Build a saving culture and facilitate credit with a fair interest rate for members.
